Shipping Options & Delivery Times

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email.

Standard Shipping

  • Estimated delivery: 3-5 business days
  • Flat rate: ₹499
  • Free on orders over ₹4,000

Express Shipping

  • Estimated delivery: 1-2 business days
  • Flat rate: ₹999
  • Order by 1 PM EST for same-day processing

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. Delivery times and costs vary by destination. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Processing

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. Please allow 24 hours for the tracking information to update in the carrier's system.

Shipping Restrictions

Some items may have shipping restrictions based on your location. These restrictions will be noted on the product page.

Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us as undeliverable, we will contact you to confirm your shipping address. Additional shipping fees may apply to reship the order.

Need help with your order?

If you have any questions about shipping or need assistance with your order, please don't hesitate to contact us.
